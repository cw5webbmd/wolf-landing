What WOLF Stands For

WOLF embodies our mission to make estate planning accessible, affordable, and deeply meaningful for everyday Americans.

W — Wealth Creation & Preservation: Build and protect what you’ve earned so it continues to grow for generations.

O — Ownership Transfer: Ensure your assets pass seamlessly to those you love with clarity and confidence.

L — Legacy Building: Establish and cement your legacy through intentional planning that reflects your values.

F — Family Protection: Safeguard your family’s future, keeping them secure, supported, and unified when it matters most.

The founder of WOLF, a U.S. Army veteran with 30 years of service, created this platform out of a deep respect for everyday Americans. Having spent a lifetime leading, serving, and protecting others, he understands the importance of stability, preparation, and family. WOLF was built to help hardworking people create wealth, preserve ownership, and protect their legacy—without the high costs or complexity of traditional legal services.
